HOW TO PLAY

OBJECTIVE: Defeat all aliens and the MOTHER SPACE boss!

CONTROLS:

• Arrow Left/Right - Move your ship

• SPACE (tap) - Fire bullets (max 3 at once)

• SPACE (hold 5 sec) - MEGA LASER BEAM!

• P or Pause Button - Pause/Resume game

LASER SUPERPOWER:

• Hold SPACE for 5 seconds to charge

• Fires devastating laser beam!

• Destroys everything in its path!

• 5 second cooldown after use

• Watch the power meter at the bottom!

ENEMIES:

• Red aliens = 30 points

• Orange aliens = 20 points

• Yellow aliens = 10 points

• MOTHER SPACE (Level 3) = 1000 points + victory!

COMBO SYSTEM:

• Hit enemies quickly for combo bonuses!

• Each combo adds 50% bonus points

GAME PROGRESSION:

• Complete 3 levels to win

• Level 3: Epic boss fight!

• Boss has 50 HP - watch the health bar!
